Kevin James Molduene

I have been blessed to live an interesting life filled with travel,
I have been to all areas of North America (and even got kicked out of
Europe)I have had jobs ranging from a tour bus driver in Alaska, a cruise
ship DJ in the Caribbean, to a casino pit boss in Washington. As an entrepreneur
I ran a successful party planning business, which led to becoming a politico working
on three campaigns (2 Senate, 1 Presidential). Eventually, I opened my own full scale
restaurant serving up delicious Mediterranean foods (Feta in Ocala, FL). Throughout all
these journeys I have always had a personal creative spark that I embellished with either
writing or performance. Having a syndicated humor column in the Spokesman-Review and
performing on stage in front of hundreds at notable venues like, Ocala Civic Theatre.
Last year my personal ambitions finally took me to New York City to pursue a career as an actor.

In addition to acting, i'm also an avid gamer having created my own card game, "Regicide", you
can see it by going to the page or link here, www.regicidethegame.com
